Hello, my name is Devanne Aceves and I am a web development student at Lubbock Coding Academy.

I grew up in Midland, Texas - a small, but somewhat overpopulated city. I lived with my grandparents, my mother, and my little brother. I attribute my family-oriented personality to this.

A few months before I turned 19, I moved to Lubbock, Texas, to live with my wonderful fiancé. We have a shih tzu and an african pygmy hedgehog - very fun!

I attended Early College High School at Midland College, where I earned my associate's degree while I completed high school. Most college courses I took were dual credit and it was a very heavy workload. My senior year, I ended up completing more than 10 classes at a time for high school and college. This was also where I got my first part-time job. I worked as a math tutor at Midland College's Math Lab where I would work with students in middle school, high school, and college. I would help students with developmental math, geometry, algebra, pre-calculus, and trigonometry. I graduated in May of 2017.
