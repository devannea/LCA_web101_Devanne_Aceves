HOW TO PLAY:


  • Player 1 is X; Player 2 is O.
  • Player 1 goes first and will place an X in one of the squares.
  • Player 2 goes after Player 1 and will place an O in one of the squares.
  • Continue to take turns until a player is declared the winner by placing 3 X's or O's in a column, row, or diagnal line.
